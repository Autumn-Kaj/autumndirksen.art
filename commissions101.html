How to Commission Me

  1. To get started, visit my Etsy shop, Autumns Art Store. From the main shop page, select “contact” or “request custom order”. (Both of these work just fine).
  2. Next you will be asked to sign in or create an Etsy account. If you are already signed in, you will be directed to either my messages or to the “request custom order” page.
  3. When you reach either page, please have a few things ready to share with me:
    1. The type of commission you would like
    2. Photo(s) or drawing reference(s)
    3. Any ideas that you have for the artwork (examples include mood or character personality, if you want a complex or simple background, or any props etc.)
  4. After we have discussed what you would like, I will be able to give an estimate on how long the commission will take, and how much it will cost. Throughout the process I will send updates and ask for opinions.
  5. Lastly, when it comes time to purchase, I will ensure that you are ready to purchase before listing your artwork as a digital download listing. Digital download listings allow you to purchase like any other listing on Etsy. However, after you purchase, you will be able to download your artwork files directly to your computer from Etsy.
